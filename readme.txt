
Reign of Grelok

Commands:
- movement: n(orth), s(outh), e(ast), w(est)
- inventory: i(nventory)
- look at thing: e(xamine), l(ook at)
- pick up thing: t(ake), p(ick up)
- drop thing: d(rop)

- communication with npcs: talk to (tt), ask [npc] (about) [thing]
- general use of items: use [thing] (on) [thing|npc|monster]
- fight: attack|slay [monster] ((with) [thing]), use [thing] (on) [monster]

- other: read [thing], search [thing]
